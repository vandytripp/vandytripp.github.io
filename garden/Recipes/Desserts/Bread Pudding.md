6 slices of bread, broken into small pieces and kinda stale
5 cups milk
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup heavy cream
5 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
Pinch of salt
About 2 tbsp butter, cut into 5-6 small pieces
Sprinkle of nutmeg and cinnamon

Preheat oven at 425.

Combine all ingredients except butter, nutmeg, and cinnamon. Pour into greased pan. Put pieces butter on top (about 1/4 tbsp each). Sprinkle with nutmeg and cinnamon. Bake for 50 min.
