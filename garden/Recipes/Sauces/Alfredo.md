5 cloves garlic

2 cups heavy cream

5 tbsp butter

4 oz cream cheese

3/4 cup Parmesan

1 tsp salt

1/2 tsp pepper

1/2 tsp herbs de Provence

 

Melt butter over medium-low heat.
•	Add minced garlic and sauté for 30–45 seconds, until fragrant (don’t brown it).
•	Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer for 2–3 minutes.
•	Stir in Parmesan and cream cheese a handful at a time, whisking until melted and smooth.
•	Season with salt, black pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg or red pepper if using.
