4 boneless chicken breasts (cooked and diced)
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 cup mayo
1 cup sour cream
1 bag frozen broccoli (cook and drain)
1 box cheezits (crushed)

Preheat oven at 350. 

Layer chicken in 9x13 pan with broccoli on top. Mix soup, mayo, and sour cream; spread atop broccoli. Spread crushed cheezits on top. 

Bake for 45 minutes.
