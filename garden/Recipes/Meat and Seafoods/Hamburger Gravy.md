1 lb ground beef
1/2 stick butter
3 tbsp flour
1 can evaporated milk (and one can water and two cans milk)
1 tbsp salt
1 tsp pepper
2 tsp beef bouillon (more to taste)

Cook ground beef and drain. Push to side of pan. In the same pan, melt butter and add flour to make a paste. Mix paste into ground beef. Add remaining ingredients and cook until thick.
