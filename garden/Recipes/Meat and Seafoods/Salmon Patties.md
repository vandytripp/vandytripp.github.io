2 cans salmon, drained and deboned
1/2 cup cornmeal
1/4 cup breadcrumbs (Italian preferred)
3 eggs, mixed
1/4 cup mayo
1/4 tsp garlic and onion powder

Mix everything together and form into patties. (If too dry, add mayo. If too wet, cornmeal). Fry in oil/butter for 3-5 minutes on each side until golden brown. 

Serve with a mayo-sriracha sauce. get 1-2 cups mayo and add sriracha sauce (about 3 tbsp) until medium pink.
