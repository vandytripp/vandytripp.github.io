1 cup butter
3/4 cup sugar
3 large overripe bananas (or 4 medium), mashed
2 eggs
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda

Combine butter (room temp/melted) and sugar. Add bananas and eggs. Mix well. Add flour and soda. 

Bake at 325 for 1 hour and 15 min in a greased loaf pan (or 65 minutes if using a wider, non-loaf pan)
